QUICK START GUIDE - Predictive Pulse v2.0
=========================================

## Installation (One-time setup)

1. Install dependencies:
   pip install -r requirements.txt

2. Run the application:
   python app.py

3. Open your browser:
   http://localhost:10000

## First Time Use

1. Click "Register" to create a new account
   - Username: Choose any username
   - Email: Your email address
   - Password: At least 6 characters

2. Click "Login" and enter your credentials

3. You're now logged in! You can:
   - Make predictions (Predict button)
   - View your history (Dashboard button)
   - Logout when done

## Making a Prediction

1. Click "Predict"
2. Fill out the 4-section form:
   - Section 1: Personal Information (Age, Gender)
   - Section 2: Medical History (Hypertension history, Medication, etc.)
   - Section 3: Symptoms & Severity (Shortness of breath, Vision changes, etc.)
   - Section 4: Blood Pressure & Optional Metrics (Systolic, Diastolic, Height, Weight)
3. Click "Get Prediction"

## Understanding Your Results

- **Blood Pressure Stage**: Color-coded (Green=Normal, Orange=Stage-1, Red=Stage-2, Dark Red=Crisis)
- **Confidence Score**: How sure the model is (0-100%)
- **Risk Score**: Your overall health risk (0-100)
- **Recommendations**: Personalized health advice based on severity

## Dashboard Features

- View all your predictions
- See trends over time
- Click "View" for detailed information
- Click "PDF" to download report
- Click "Delete" to remove prediction

## PDF Export

- Export any prediction as a professional PDF report
- Share with your doctor
- Includes all health data and recommendations

## Mobile Support

- App works on phones, tablets, and desktops
- All features available on mobile
- Touch-friendly interface

## Important Notes

- Height/Weight/Heart Rate are optional
- Data is securely stored in database
- Only you can see your predictions
- Predictions are only suggestions - consult a doctor for medical advice

## Troubleshooting

Database not created?
  - Delete predictive_pulse.db
  - Restart app

Can't login after registering?
  - Make sure password matches
  - Check email for typos

Pages showing errors?
  - Make sure all .html files are in templates/ folder
  - Restart the app

Model loading error?
  - Ensure model.joblib is in project root
  - Check file is not corrupted
